Canon AE-1


Authenticity 100%
Description

The Canon AE-1 is a classic 35mm film SLR camera known for its iconic design and user-friendly features. It was first introduced in 1976 and became immensely popular among photographers for its innovative automatic exposure capabilities, making it a beloved choice for both amateurs and professionals.
